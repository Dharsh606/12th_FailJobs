Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about 12thFailJobs

👷 For Workers

How do I create a worker account? +

Creating a worker account is simple and free:

  1. Click on "I am a Worker" on the homepage
  2. Fill in your basic details (name, email, phone)
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile with skills and experience
  5. Start applying for jobs!
Tip: Complete profiles get 3x more responses from employers.
Is it really free for workers? +

Yes! All basic features for workers are completely free:

  • ✅ Profile creation and management
  • ✅ Job search and browsing
  • ✅ Job applications (unlimited)
  • ✅ Direct messaging with employers
  • ✅ Application status tracking

Premium features like profile highlighting are available but optional.

How do I apply for a job? +

Applying for jobs is quick and easy:

  1. Search for jobs using filters (location, job type, skills)
  2. Click on any job to view details
  3. Click "Apply Now" button
  4. Add a brief message to the employer (optional)
  5. Submit your application

You can track all your applications in the "My Applications" section.

What types of jobs are available? +

We offer a wide range of job opportunities across various sectors:

  • Construction: Laborers, carpenters, electricians, plumbers
  • Manufacturing: Factory workers, machine operators, quality control
  • Retail: Sales staff, cashiers, store assistants
  • Hospitality: Waiters, cooks, housekeeping, security
  • Delivery: Drivers, delivery partners, logistics
  • Services: Cleaning, gardening, maintenance, repairs
How do I know if a job is legitimate? +

We verify all recruiters, but you should still be careful:

  • Look for the "Verified Recruiter" badge
  • Check the recruiter's profile and company details
  • Read reviews from other workers
  • Never pay money to get a job
  • Meet in safe, public places for interviews
Warning: If anyone asks for money for job placement, report them immediately.

👔 For Recruiters

How do I post a job? +

Posting a job is straightforward:

  1. Log in to your recruiter account
  2. Click "Post New Job" button
  3. Fill in job details (title, description, location, salary)
  4. Select required skills and qualifications
  5. Choose job visibility (free or featured)
  6. Publish the job

Jobs are typically live within minutes of posting.
